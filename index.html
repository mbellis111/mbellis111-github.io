The Brand

Consumer demand for American whiskey has exploded over the last few years, resulting in a “billion-dollar bourbon boom.” Since bourbon must be aged in new barrels, the surge in demand has left behind millions of used barrels.

Companies have found creative ways to re-purpose these barrels as furnishings, creating a new demand for barrel-themed items. You’ve likely seen bourbon barrels in bars or liquor stores, however most people can’t fit full-sized barrels in their homes. We developed the idea to package our bottles inside mini-barrels, so that you can now bring a rustic bourbon barrel into your own home.

The barrel is also our signature, serving as a promise to provide consistently good products.

Our Products

All Bellis products are designed to deliver a smooth experiance without any harsh bite or burn.

The original Bellis Bourbon undergos a second distillation process to remove impurities for a smooth finish. This proprietary process separates out the fusel alcohols from the flavor, color, and aroma, making the whiskey easy to drink.

Batch #1 of Bellis Bourbon is distilled with wheat instead of rye to create a smooth and creamy flavor. It is aged for over four years and bottled at 90 proof, with notes of both vanilla and caramel.

The tequila used in Bellis Blue Agave is imported from Jalisco, Mexico and infused with cane-neutral spirits to eliminate any “tequila burn”. It is aged for over two months in Bellis Bourbon barrels and bottled at 80 proof.

About Us

Bellis Bourbon is the product of a 15+ year friendship.

Colin Santacroce is a veteran of two combat tours to Afghanistan in the United States Army. As a scholarship recipient to Syracuse University, Colin tended bar and quickly realized that customers appreciate bourbon with a smooth finish, but few brands could consistently deliver such a product.

Mike Bellis works for a Fortune 500 company in the financial services industry. Mike’s business experience helped turn Colin’s dream of an innovative bourbon product into the reality that is Bellis Bourbon.

When you see the Bellis Bourbon barrel, you can expect a consistently smooth bourbon from the first sip to the last drop.

Signature Drinks

Bellis Bourbon Sour

  • 3 oz. Bellis Bourbon
  • 1 oz. lemon juice
  • ½ oz. simple syrup
  • ½ oz. egg whites
Combine ingredients and shake vigorously. Pour over ice into a rocks glass.

Bellis Cure All

  • 3 ounces Bellis Bourbon
  • 2 oz. Beet Juice
  • 1 oz. Lemon Juice
Combine ingredients and shake vigorously. Best served with ice.

Bellis Burro

  • 2 oz. Bellis Blue Agave
  • 4 oz. ginger beer
  • ½ oz. lime juice
Combine hot water and honey, shake vigorously to dissolve. Pour all ingredients over ice into a highball glass.
