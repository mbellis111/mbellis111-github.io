Our Product

Consumer demand for bourbon has exploded over the last few years, resulting in a “billion-dollar bourbon boom.” Since bourbon must be aged in new barrels, the surge in demand has left behind millions of used bourbon barrels. Companies have found creative ways to repurpose these barrels as furnishings, creating a new demand for barrel-themed items. You’ve likely seen a bourbon barrel in a bar or liquor store; however, most people can’t put full-sized barrels in their homes. We developed the idea to package our bottles inside mini bourbon barrels, so that you can now bring a rustic, bourbon barrel into your home.

The barrel is also our signature, serving as our promise to provide a consistently good product.

The Process

Unlike other young bourbons, which are typically harsh, Bellis Bourbon undergoes a second distillation process to remove impurities for a smooth finish. This proprietary process separates out the fusel oils from the flavor, color, and aroma, making the bourbon easy to drink.

Fusel oils - German for “bad liquor”- are a mixture of several alcohols which are produced as a byproduct of alcohol fermentation. These oils give spirit products a harsh rubbing alcohol taste and may contribute to hangover symptoms. Bourbon is prone to having excessive concentrations of fusel oils, but unlike other bourbons, Bellis Bourbon is so smooth, you’ll want a double.

About Us

Bellis Bourbon is the product of a fourteen year friendship.

Colin Santacroce is a veteran of two combat tours to Afghanistan in the United States Army. As a scholarship recipient to Syracuse University, Colin tended bar and quickly realized that customers appreciate bourbon with a smooth finish, but few brands could consistently deliver such a product.

Mike Bellis works for a Fortune 500 company in the financial services industry. Mike’s business experience helped turn Colin’s dream of an innovative bourbon product into the reality that is Bellis Bourbon.

When you see the Bellis Bourbon barrel, you can expect a consistently smooth bourbon from the first sip to the last drop.

Signature Drinks

Bellis Boulevardier

In a chilled glass:

  • 1.5 ounces Bellis Bourbon
  • 1 ounce Campari
  • 1 ounce sweet vermouth

Smoked Old Fashioned

Combine with ice with a cocktail shaker:

  • 2 oz. Bellis Bourbon
  • 2 dashes Orange bitters
  • 1 tbsp Vermont Smoked Maple Syrup
  • 1 tsp Maraschino Cherry Syrup
  • Juice from 1 slice Muddled Orange
  • 1 splash Water

Bellis Bee's Knees

  • 2.5 ounce Bellis Bourbon
  • 1 lemon freshly pressed
  • 1/2 ounce honey
  • 1/2 ounce boiling water
Combine boiling water and honey, shake vigorously to combine. Pour all ingredients into highball glass over ice, serve.
